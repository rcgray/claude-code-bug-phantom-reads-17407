  Severity: Low — This is a criteria definition issue, not a design flaw.

  - Performance telemetry (sampling acceptable)

  queries per Requirement 4.5 require complete traces)                          
                                                                                
  Severity: Medium — The mitigation as written could lead implementers to sample
   audit events, causing compliance violations.
